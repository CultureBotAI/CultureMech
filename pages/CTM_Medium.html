Preparation Protocol

  1. MIX
    CTM medium basis:
  2. AUTOCLAVE
    Mix all ingredients in 1.0 L medium bottle. Add distilled water close to 1.0 L. Adjust pH to 7.0 with 2 M KOH and fill up to 1.0 L. Autoclave at 121C for 40 min. After autoclaving, seal medium bottle immediately in order to avoid oxygenation. Cool down to around 70C. Add 30 ml filter-sterilized Mixed solution (see below) to the autoclaved CTM medium basis for completing the CTM medium.
  3. MIX
    Add distilled water to bring final volume to 30 ml.
