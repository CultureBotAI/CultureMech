Preparation Protocol

  1. AUTOCLAVE
    Prepare and autoclave medium 461 (vitamins not necessary). Add 10 ml of pyrrolic acid solution per liter mineral medium.
  2. AUTOCLAVE
    Dissolve and autoclave the phosphates separately. Add the vitamin solution and trace element solution after autoclaving. After mixing, pH should be 7.5.
  3. MIX
    Rehydrate and cultivate the lyophilized cells in complex medium recommended for the specific strain (e.g. medium 1, 220 or 535) first. After this reactivation, cultivate on mineral medium 461 with the appropriate carbon source.
  4. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Dissolve 10 mol pyrrole-2-carboxylic acid per liter potassium phosphate buffer (50 mM; pH 7.2) and sterilize the solution by membrane filtration.
