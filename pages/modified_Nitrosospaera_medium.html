Preparation Protocol

  1. MIX
    Incubate all used glass ware and magnetic stirring rods in 0.1 M HCl and rinse 3x with double distilled water to remove residual detergents (always check visual for soap bubbles).
  2. AUTOCLAVE
    Autoclave the Basic-FWM and cool to room temperature. This medium should be free of any precipitations!
  3. STORE
    Aseptically, add per L of BASIC-FWM the following solutions from sterile stocks (do not store complemented FWM): - Trace element solution (Nitrososphaera) - FeNaEDTA solution - NaHCO3 solution - HEPES buffer solution - NH4Cl solution - Sodiumpyruvate solution
  4. ADJUST_PH
    Adjust pH to 7.5.
  5. HEAT
    This solution contains no iron! Sterilise by autoclaving or filtration (0.2 µm) and store in the dark at 4 °C.
  6. ADJUST_PH
    Fist dissolve NaOH in ca 600 ml water, then add HEPES, adjust pH to 7.5 and fill up to 1000 ml volume.
  7. HEAT
    Sterilise by filtration (0.2 µm) and store in the dark at 4 °C.
  8. DISSOLVE
    Dissolve 4.2 g of NaHCO3 in 50 mL Double distilled water (use a small beaker with stirring rod to dissolve) and sterilize by filtration (0.2 µm) in the laminar flow hood. Store in a sterile 50 mL falcon tube at 4 °C. Avoid high headspace to liquid ratios to ensure stable concentration.
  9. DISSOLVE
    Dissolve 2.67 g ammonium chloride in 50 mL Double distilled water.
  10. MIX
    Filtrate (0.2 µm).
  11. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Dissolve 5.5 g in 50 mL and filter it through 0.2 µm sterile filter in a sterile falcon tube. Store at 4 °C
