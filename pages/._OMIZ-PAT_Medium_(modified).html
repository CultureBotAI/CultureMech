Preparation Protocol

  1. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Add 10 ml rabbit serum, flush the medium with N2CO2, adjust the pH to 6.9 and filter-sterilize the completed medium into tubes.
  2. HEAT
    This recipe describes the preparation of 200 ml medium, which can be used for about 4 weeks when kept at 4°C.
  3. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Solutions A, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -20°C
  4. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Solutions B, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -20°C
  5. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Solutions C, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -20°C
  6. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Solutions D, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -20°C
  7. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Solutions E, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -20°C
  8. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Solution F, flush with nitrogen to make it anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at 4°C
  9. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Solutions G, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -20°C
  10. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Solutions H, flush each with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -20°C
  11. HEAT
    Prepare Solution I and store at -20°C
  12. HEAT
    D,L-alpha-lipoic acid (1 mg/ml); in 2-mercaptoethanol-ethanol (1:10); store at -20°C
  13. MIX
    Hemin (0.2 mM) in NaOH (10 mM)
  14. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Homogenize 42 g fresh baker yeast in 140 ml distilled water, incubate for 24h at 56°C and centrifuge to harvest the supernatant. Adjust pH to 7 with NaOH, make anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -80°C
  15. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Trace elements I, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at 4°C
  16. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Trace elements II, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at 4°C
  17. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Trace elements III, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at 4°C
  18. MIX
    FeSO4 x 7H2O (10 mM) in HCl (10 mM)
  19. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Supplements 1, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -20°C
  20. MIX
    Ascorbic acid (10%; w/v); in distilled water, neutralize with NaOH
  21. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Supplements 2, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -20°C
  22. MIX
    Glutathion (0.5 M); in distilled water, neutralize with NaOH
  23. FILTER_STERILIZE
    Prepare Supplements 3, flush with nitrogen to make them anoxic, filter-sterilize and store at -20°C
  24. HEAT
    Cholesterol (1 mg/ml); in Ethanol (100%); store at -20°C
  25. HEAT
    NaHCO3 (10%; w/v) in distilled water, store at 4°C
