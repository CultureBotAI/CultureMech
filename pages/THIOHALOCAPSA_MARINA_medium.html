Preparation Protocol

  1. AUTOCLAVE
    Prepare the medium without the NaHCO3, Na2S x 9 H2O, Na2S2O3 x 5 H2O and vitamin B12, under a nitrogen atmosphere dispense into tubes or bottles fitted with rubber stoppers so that they are half full. Autoclave and add the vitamin B12 from a filter-sterilised stock solution and the NaHCO3, Na2S x 9 H2O, Na2S2O3 x 5 H2O from sterile stock solutions. The final pH of the medium should be 7.2.
  2. MIX
    For Allochromatium phaeobacterium DSM 19781 reduce the amount of NaCl to 10.0 g/l and increase the amount of NH4Cl to 0.64 g/l.
  3. ADJUST_PH
    Adjust pH to 6.8.
