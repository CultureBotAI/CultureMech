Preparation Protocol

  1. MIX
    For f/10 medium for calcifying Emiliania, simply dilute all stock solutions by 1/5th.
  2. AUTOCLAVE
    Make up to 1 litre with filtered natural seawater. Adjust pH to 8.0 with 1M NaOH or 1 M HCl prior to autoclaving. For agar add 15g per litre Bacteriological Agar. Autoclave at 15 psi for 15 minutes.
  3. MIX
    First make primary stocks of Cyanocobalamin and Biotin.
  4. POUR_PLATES
    Dispense any excess primary stocks into 1 ml aliquots and freeze.
