Preparation Protocol

  1. MIX
    Bubble the medium with nitrogen gas and fill 10 ml in 15 ml tubes with a rubber septum under a stream of nitrogen gas.
  2. AUTOCLAVE
    Autoclave at 121 °C for 15 min.
  3. MIX
    Fill in 100ml serum bottle, bubble with CO2 for approx. 15 min, then close tightly.
  4. AUTOCLAVE
    Autoclave in closed vessel under CO2 atmosphere.
  5. AUTOCLAVE
    Autoclave at 121 °C for 15 min.
